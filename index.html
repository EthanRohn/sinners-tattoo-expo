FAQ

Do I need to book an artist ahead of time?

You can and a lot of people do, follow your favorites on Instagram and DM them to lock in a slot before the expo. But tons of artists also take walk-ups all weekend. Either way works.

DCan I come just to watch?

Yep. Plenty of people do. Walking the floor watching world-class artists work is worth the price of admission on its own.

Is this kid-friendly?

Yes! Kids 12 and under get in free with a paying adult. Just know the energy gets louder at night, and the burlesque shows are adults-only.

Can I buy tickets at the door?

Yes, but online is cheaper and faster. Door prices go up and tickets have sold out before, don't risk it.

What's the refund policy?

Tickets are non-refundable but transferable. Can't make it? Send a friend.

I've never been to a tattoo convention. Will I feel out of place?

No. A big chunk of the crowd every year is first-timers, and honestly, there's no better place to join the community or get your first piece than surrounded by 600+ of the best artists in the world.
